# Financial Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview
This project is a Power BI dashboard built to analyze overall financial performance across regions, products, and sales channels.  
It focuses on revenue, cost, profit, and margin trends to support business decision-making.

![Dashboard Preview](screenshots/dashboard_overview.png)

----

## Key Metrics
- Total Revenue
- Total Cost
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin

All metrics can be filtered by **region**, **year**, and **product category**.

---

## Dashboard Highlights
- Monthly revenue trend analysis
- Revenue and profit breakdown by region
- Product category performance comparison
- Sales channel distribution (Online vs Retail)
- Top performing sales representatives
- Clear business insight highlighting negative profit margins

---

## Key Insights
- Total costs exceed revenue across all product categories, resulting in negative profit margins
- Regional performance varies, with consistent losses across regions
- Certain product categories generate higher revenue but still operate at a loss
- Cost control and pricing strategy require immediate attention
